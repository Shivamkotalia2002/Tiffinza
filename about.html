OUR STORY


Tiffinza was established in 2022 with the goal of bringing tiffin services together in one place to make it easier for you to find the best options to suit your needs. Our platform offers a diverse selection of tiffin services, each with their unique speciality and flavors.

We understand that busy lifestyles and hectic schedules often make it challenging to find the time to cook meals, which is why we have created a one-stop-shop for tiffin services. Whether you are a student , a working professional, or a homemaker, we have something for everyone.

Our platform features tiffin services that cater to a range of dietary preferences, including vegetarian, non-vegetarian, and vegan options. We believe in providing our customers with the highest quality food that is both healthy and delicious.

We take pride in offering a seamless ordering experience, and our user-friendly website makes it easy to browse and order your favorite tiffin service. We also prioritize timely deliveries to ensure that you receive your meals fresh and on time.

We are passionate about providing you with a range of options and supporting local businesses. We strive to create a community of tiffin service providers who are committed to delivering exceptional food and customer service.
